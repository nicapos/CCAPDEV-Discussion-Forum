Good Things Happen after 2:00 AM

alpha123

May 10, 2022

I’m a solid fan of how i met your mother, however, there was this one rough night when everything went wrong; I failed an exam, scolded by my parents, and got “friendzoned”. I was very sad and frustrated that night so I went out for a walk and a coffee. It was already 2:00am, i was thinking of going home already when some stranger approached me and asked if I could guard her stuff and she’s going to the restroom lang. She is cute so i said okay, when she came back, we had a little chat and the rest is history. Today is our 1st anniversary and I couldn’t be any happier! Lesson: something good can still happen after 2:00am! PS: I couldn’t wait to tell my future kids how I met their mother (or aunt ) lol
